## Project Specifications Document
### Document ID: MHRC-SPEC-2024-15

## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

**Project Name:** Metro Heights Residential Complex
**Location:** 1250 Downtown Boulevard, Metro City
**Client:** Urban Development Corporation
**Architect:** Steinberg & Associates Architecture
**Structural Engineer:** Matthews Structural Engineering LLC
**Project Manager:** Sarah Chen, P.E.

**Project Scope:**
- 28-story residential tower with mixed-use ground floor
- 420 residential units (studio, 1BR, 2BR, 3BR configurations)
- 15,000 sq ft commercial retail space
- 3-level underground parking garage (480 spaces)
- Rooftop amenity deck with pool and recreation facilities

---

## 2. STRUCTURAL DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

**Foundation System:**
- Drilled pier foundation extending 65 feet to bearing stratum
- 48-inch diameter reinforced concrete piers
- Grade beam system connecting pier caps
- Waterproofing: polymer-modified bituminous membrane

**Superstructure:**
- Cast-in-place reinforced concrete frame construction
- Concrete strength: f'c = 4,000 psi (floors 1-15), f'c = 5,000 psi (floors 16-28)
- Rebar: Grade 60 (fy = 60,000 psi)
- Post-tensioned concrete slabs: 8-inch typical thickness
- Shear walls for lateral resistance, designed for Zone 3 seismic requirements

**Steel Components:**
- Structural steel: ASTM A992 Grade 50
- Welding: E70XX electrodes, AWS D1.1 procedures
- Fireproofing: spray-applied fire resistive materials (SFRM) for 2-hour rating

## 4. MECHANICAL SYSTEMS

**HVAC Design:**
- Variable refrigerant flow (VRF) system for residential units
- Energy recovery ventilators (ERV) for fresh air
- Rooftop units for commercial spaces and common areas
- BMS control system with web-based interface

**Plumbing Systems:**
- Domestic water: copper piping with lead-free solder
- Waste systems: cast iron soil pipe with no-hub joints
- Water heaters: high-efficiency condensing units
- Fire suppression: wet sprinkler system throughout

**Electrical Systems:**
- Service: 4000A, 480/277V, 3-phase main switchboard
- Emergency generator: 750kW diesel unit with automatic transfer
- Lighting: LED fixtures throughout, daylight harvesting controls
- Data/communications: structured cabling system, fiber backbone

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION TIMELINE

**Phase 1 - Site Preparation:** 3 months
- Demolition of existing structures
- Utility relocations and temporary services
- Site excavation and dewatering systems

**Phase 2 - Foundation Work:** 4 months
- Drilled pier installation
- Grade beam and basement slab construction
- Below-grade waterproofing and backfill

**Phase 3 - Superstructure:** 18 months
- Concrete frame construction (floors 1-28)
- Exterior envelope installation
- Roofing and weatherproofing

**Phase 4 - Building Systems:** 8 months
- MEP rough-in and testing
- Interior framing and drywall
- Flooring and finish installations

**Phase 5 - Final Closeout:** 3 months
- Punch list completion
- Final inspections and certifications
- Owner training and warranty documentation

**Total Project Duration:** 36 months
**Substantial Completion:** December 2027

---

## 6. QUALITY CONTROL REQUIREMENTS

**Materials Testing:**
- Concrete: cylinder tests every 100 cubic yards
- Structural steel: mill certificates and weld testing per AWS D1.1
- Masonry: prism testing for compressive strength
- Soils: compaction testing at 95% standard Proctor density

**Inspection Requirements:**
- Special inspections per IBC Chapter 17
- Third-party commissioning for all MEP systems
- Envelope air leakage testing per ASTM E779
- Structural steel welding inspection: 100% visual, 10% UT testing
